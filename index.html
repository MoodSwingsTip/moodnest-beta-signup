MoodNest Flip Cards 🌈

👀 Name 5 things you can see
🌼 You’re grounded in the now. You’re doing great.
👂 Name 4 things you can hear
🎧 You are present. Your senses are helping you feel safe.
✋ Touch 3 things around you
🧸 Soft textures calm your body. You’re safe.
👃 Smell 2 things
🌸 Breathing helps your body relax. You’re okay.
👅 Taste 1 thing or imagine your favorite food
🍓 Your body is coming back to calm. You’re in control.
🧘 Take 3 slow belly breaths
🌬️ Inhale peace… exhale the worry. You’re strong.
🤲 Rub your hands together
🔥 Feel the warmth? That’s your power returning.
👣 Wiggle your toes and feet
🌈 You’re grounded. You’re safe right here.
🎵 Hum your favorite song
🎤 Your voice is calm. You’re bringing comfort to yourself.
🧸 Hold your favorite stuffed animal or soft item
🫶 It’s okay to need comfort. You’re doing the right thing.
⏸️ Press your palms together firmly
🧩 This gives your brain a rest and resets your heart.
🖐️ Trace your fingers while breathing in and out
🌿 You’re finding your calm. One breath at a time.
🤗 Give yourself a tight hug
💛 You are loved. You are safe. You are enough.
👋 Shake out your hands like raindrops
💦 Let the stress fall away. You’re in control again.
💭 Whisper 3 kind things to yourself
✨ You deserve gentle words and calm feelings.
🎈 Imagine a balloon floating your worry away
🎈 Let it go… let it rise… let peace return.
🔔 Tap gently on your chest
💗 Tapping calms your nervous system. Feel the rhythm of safety.
👁️ Close your eyes for 10 seconds
🌙 You are resting. Everything is slowing down.
🐢 Move like a slow turtle for 30 seconds
🐢 Slowing down helps your brain feel safe.
🔄 Count backward from 10
⏳ Counting gives your mind something peaceful to hold.
📦 Imagine putting your worry in a box
📪 The box is closed. You’ll open it only when ready.
📸 Picture your happiest memory
🎉 That memory is your anchor. You’re still that joyful you.
🧠 Say: “This is a feeling. It will pass.”
🌥️ You’re not the feeling. You’re bigger than the moment.
🎨 Draw or color how you feel
🖍️ Creativity helps your emotions find a way out.
🛑 Say out loud: “STOP” to interrupt the panic
🟢 Now breathe. You are strong and in charge.
